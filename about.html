The HoCater Games provides people with popular games made by the game developers.

We are only a small indie team of only 12 staff members, but we will provide our customers to the best of our abilities.


Our company started during the pandemic in 2020, and as people were required to work from home and are unable to go out,

we cater to those people who are isolated and are unable to head outside.

It is for this exact reason that we started the company, just so that we can lighten up people's day with entertainment online!


The utmost importance of the company is its services to the customers, which are you!

As we continue to grow bigger each day, prioritising our customer support is our main goal.
